Overview

Sensible Portions is the leading organic healthy snack food brand in the US and Canada, with a wide product catalogue ranging from potato chips to pita crackers with kosher and gluten-free options.

The brand was looking for a fun website redesign geared towards a younger audience, to reflect their positioning as the family friendly choice for healthy eating on-the-go.

Brief

The client was in need of some help with branding and art direction in order to visually align their product range cohesively and make the website look distinctively theirs.

They wanted to be able to edit content and add new snacks to their product range quickly and easily using a custom built content management system. A store locator, customer contact hub and search mechanism were also required.

Outcome

I took the lead on the redesign, liaising directly with the client and dev team in an agile working environment. The fully-responsive site contains 10+ pages including a media centre, article listings and product detail pages, all modifiable client-side using django CMS.

The new art direction takes a flat design approach using bright colours and vector illustrations to bring the products to life. I created custom UI elements including hand-drawn icons and 3D multi-state buttons.

Custom UI Elements

All of the icons on the site are custom designed, right down to the arrows on the carousels. I tried to give the nutritional icons a hand-drawn aesthetic by using diagonal gradients and rugged lines.
